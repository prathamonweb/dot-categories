A motor carrier permit is mandatory to operate legally across the United States. It is an interstate operating authority that serves as evidence of registration with the Department of Motor Vehicles. Aspire DOT can assist you in obtaining your motor carrier permit and reduce your stress.

How to apply online

Trucking companies must apply for truck permits online through the DMV website before operating. Documents needed for the registration include Carrier Identification Number, motor carrier application form, proof of insurance, and EPN Number.

Benefits of motor carrier renewal

Operating a trucking company is a long process that requires a legal permit. It allows you to conduct business peacefully without the authorities being on your neck. Here are some benefits of renewing your trucking permit:

Prevents penalties

Operating across the United States without permits is a severe offense against the law. Motor carriers are stopped on the road from time to time to check their permit and failure to produce a valid one attracts punishments. These include fines, penalties, license revoke, and many more. Renewing your truck permits prevents penalties and allows your business to move smoothly.

Provides safety

Permits indicate the motor carrier is roadworthy and eligible to operate commercially. That means it is safe and will not endanger the lives of other road users or freight.

Shows legitimacy

Trucking permits show your business is registered and legitimate. It implies compliance with the regulations set by the regulatory bodies in the trucking industry at the federal and state level. Without them, your company’s credibility and authenticity suffer.
